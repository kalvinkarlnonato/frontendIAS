what we do?

Vision

To promote integrity and accountability within the organization and to maintain public trust.

Mission

To conduct thorough and impartial investigations of allegations of misconduct, corruption, or other violations of organizational policies and regulations.

Core

Integrity, Impartiality, Accountability, Professionalism, Respect,

Conducting investigations

The IAS is responsible for investigating allegations of misconduct, corruption, or other violations of organizational policies and regulations. Investigations may involve gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, and making recommendations for disciplinary action.
